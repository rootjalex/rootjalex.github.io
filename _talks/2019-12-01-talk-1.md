Presentation on semester research project. We developed a compiler tool that converts existing floating point tensor pipelines in [Halide](https://halide-lang.org) to their fixed-point representations. Presentation covered the compiler tooling and numeric optimizations required to implement floating point intrinsics as variable-length fixed-point intrinsics.
